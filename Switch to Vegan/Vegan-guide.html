  • 01

    Books

    Essential reading for a plant-based mind.
    Quick Reference

    Recommended books

    A balanced shortlist across nutrition, ethics, philosophy and food systems.

    Book Author Best For Reliability Note
    Becoming Vegan (Comprehensive) Davis & Melina RD Nutrition science Most balanced and rigorous
    Vegan for Life Norris & Messina RD Practical nutrition Honest and reliable
    The Vegan Starter Kit Barnard MD Beginners Medically qualified author
    Animal Liberation Peter Singer Ethics & philosophy Academic rigour
    Eating Animals Foer Ethics & environment Well researched
    How Not to Die Greger MD Motivation & overview Read critically
    The China Study Campbell Historical context Methodology disputed
    The Omnivore's Dilemma Pollan Balanced food system view Non-vegan perspective
    Farmageddon Lymbery Industrial farming Advocacy but well reported
